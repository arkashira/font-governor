# Business Model Canvas for font-governor
## Value Proposition
font-governor offers a unified typeface governance system, empowering designers and developers to efficiently manage font file metadata, license lifecycles, and family hierarchies. The key benefits include:
* Centralized metadata management, reducing lookup time by 70%
* Automated license tracking and compliance checks, cutting audit preparation time from days to minutes
* Improved design consistency through family hierarchy management
* Seamless integration with existing Sanity-based design workflows
* Open-source and MIT-licensed for flexibility and community contribution

## Customer Segments
The primary customer segments for font-governor are:
* Creative teams and design agencies
* Software development companies with in-house design teams
* Font foundries and typeface designers
* Enterprises with large design and development departments

## Channels
font-governor will be distributed through the following channels:
* GitHub repository for open-source access and community engagement
* Sanity.io marketplace for seamless integration with Sanity-based workflows
* Vercel platform for API deployment and scalability
* Design and development conferences, meetups, and online forums for community outreach and promotion

## Revenue Streams
The revenue streams for font-governor are:
* **Support and Services**: Offer premium support, customization, and integration services for enterprises and large design agencies
* **Licensing and Partnerships**: Partner with font foundries and typeface designers to offer exclusive font packages and licensing deals
* **API and Data Services**: Provide API access and data services for font metadata, licensing, and family hierarchies to software development companies and enterprises
* **Sanity.io Marketplace**: Earn revenue through the Sanity.io marketplace for font-governor plugin sales and subscriptions

## Cost Structure
The cost structure for font-governor includes:
* **Development and Maintenance**: Ongoing development, testing, and maintenance of the font-governor codebase and Sanity schema
* **Infrastructure and Hosting**: Costs associated with hosting the font-governor API, Sanity studio, and Vercel deployment
* **Marketing and Promotion**: Expenses for community outreach, conference sponsorships, and online advertising
* **Support and Services**: Costs associated with providing premium support, customization, and integration services

## Key Resources
The key resources for font-governor are:
* **Development Team**: Experienced developers and designers familiar with Sanity, Vercel, and font governance
* **Sanity.io Partnership**: Strong partnership with Sanity.io for seamless integration and marketplace distribution
* **Font Foundry and Typeface Designer Network**: Established relationships with font foundries and typeface designers for licensing and partnership opportunities
* **Community Engagement**: Active community engagement and feedback mechanisms for ongoing improvement and development

## Key Activities
The key activities for font-governor are:
* **Development and Maintenance**: Ongoing development, testing, and maintenance of the font-governor codebase and Sanity schema

* **Partnership Development**: Establishing and maintaining partnerships with font foundries, typeface designers, and software development companies
